Zone 5

This section will be discussing zone 5. There is a lot to talk about here and this will be the longest zone specific section of this guide.
Mapping

By far, the most important part of zone 5 is mapping. Given that you are so low on resources when you start the game, opening a door into a room with a purple sarcophagus that you don't need to deal with is incredibly punishing. This is something that I've found a lot of people struggle with, but can be somewhat straightforward. Here are a few tips about mapping:
  • The exit room is 8 by 8, and is at least 7 tiles away from the starting room. All other rooms are 5 by 4.
  • There are no traps nor purple sarcophagi in the exit room. This is true* throughout the game, but knowing this is highest leverage in zone 5.
  • The door to the exit room is in the main hallway. Additionally, there are no other doors in the exit room.
  • If push comes to shove and you don't have a read on the exit, you can dig walls near doors to take a peek inside. This works well with the base shovel, as the walls will regrow.
I put together two pages: one with 5 zone 5 levels generated in the level editor so you can see patterns, and one with 6 "tests" where you are shown only what's in the hallway and you should try to figure out where the exit is. I recommend taking a look at these, especially if you aren't comfortable with zone 5 mapping.
