I am currently a postdoc at the University of Waterloo. I am looking for a new job, ideally in finance and in the GTA. My advisor was Mark Kisin.

My primary research interest is the p-adic Langlands Program, emphasizing aspects related to the Jacquet-Langlands correspondence. Recently, I have also gotten interested in some "l = p" aspects of Cohen-Lenstra heuristics.

My pronouns are she/her.
A list of all my research articles and teaching history can be found here. You can see my github, which contains some modelling that I have done, here. Additionally, I have gotten into solving 538's Riddlers, and have a compiliation of my solutions here.
